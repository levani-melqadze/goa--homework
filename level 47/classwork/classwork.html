Simple Omelette Recipe

Here is a simple and delicious omelette recipe that you can easily make at home.

Ingredients:

  • 2 large eggs
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • Optional fillings: cheese, diced vegetables, cooked meats, herbs

Instructions:

  1. Beat the eggs in a bowl, then season with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat the butter in a non-stick frying pan over medium heat.
  3. Pour in the eggs and tilt the pan to ensure they evenly coat the surface.
  4. Add your chosen fillings over one half of the omelette.
  5. Fold the omelette in half and cook for another minute, then serve hot.
