Simple Omelette Recipe

As an easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classic omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables, or meats.

Total Time: Approximately 10 minutes

Preparation: 5 minutes

Cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • Optional fillings: cheese, cooked vegetables, cooked meats, herbs

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs: In a bowl, beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper until well-mixed.
  2. Heat the pan: Over medium heat, add butter or oil.
  3. Cook the omelette: Pour the eggs into the pan, tilting to cover the surface evenly.
  4. Add fillings (optional): Sprinkle cheese or other fillings on one half of the omelette.
  5. Fold and serve: Fold the omelette in half and slide onto a plate. Serve hot!

Nutrition

Tip: For a lighter version, cook the omelette with minimal oil and skip the fillings.
